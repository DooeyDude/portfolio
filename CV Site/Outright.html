Work at Outright Games

At Outright Games I’m responsible for everything to do with Packaging. This includes making sure our titles match the First Party Templates, Discussing with Licensors, Talking to Distributors & processing their POs and Handling Rating Submissions (PEGI, ESRB, etc.). I have also helped lead the integration of our department into the company-wide Jira solution.

Gigantosaurus: Dino Kart

Gigantosaurus: Dino Kart was the first packaging project I worked on at Outright Games, supporting both the America and EMEA regions. This title marked the beginning of my experience in managing packaging workflows while simultaneously handling other active projects such as Peppa Pig: World Adventures and DC Justice League: Cosmic Chaos. Balancing multiple projects at once helped me quickly develop effective time management and cross-team coordination skills.

Peppa Pig: World Adventures

For Peppa Pig: World Adventures, I managed packaging across the America and EMEA regions, working closely with the brand owners of Peppa Pig to ensure all assets were approved to their standards. The project involved multiple revision cycles due to brand-specific requirements, making attention to detail and stakeholder communication crucial to keeping the process smooth and on schedule.

DC Justice League: Cosmic Chaos

DC Justice League: Cosmic Chaos featured a unique element—a pre-order keychain offered on specific platforms. In addition to managing the standard packaging assets for America and EMEA, I coordinated closely with platform holders to obtain approval for the promotional item. I also worked with internal teams and external vendors to source the keychains, ensuring they met quality and brand standards ahead of distribution.

Paw Patrol World

I briefly supported the packaging process for Paw Patrol World at the early stages of development before my departure from Outright Games. My involvement included initial asset reviews and regional compliance checks for America and EMEA.

The Grinch: Christmas Adventures

I assisted with the early-stage packaging setup for The Grinch: Christmas Adventures, contributing to the initial coordination and planning for the America and EMEA regions before transitioning off the project.

Rainbow High: Runway Rush

My involvement in Rainbow High: Runway Rush was limited to the beginning phases, where I provided early support on packaging preparations for America and EMEA territories prior to my departure from the company.
